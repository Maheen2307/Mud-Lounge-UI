Curiosities in Clay

Everything you need to know before stepping into the studio

do i need prior experience for the workshops?

❯

not at all! our foundation workshops are specially curated for absolute beginners. our expert artisans will guide you step-by-step through the entire process.

what should i wear to a pottery session?

❯

we recommend wearing comfortable clothes that you don't mind getting a little muddy. short nails are also highly recommended for a better grip on the wheel!

is all material included in the workshop fee?

❯

yes, the package covers everything including raw clay, premium glazes, wheel access, firing tools, and light refreshments during the break.

when can i take my finished pottery pieces home?

❯

pottery requires air drying, bisque firing, glazing, and a final kiln glaze firing. your masterpieces will be ready for pickup exactly 2 to 3 weeks after your session.

can i reschedule or cancel my workshop slot?

❯

yes, you can easily reschedule up to 48 hours before your class through your confirmation link so we can adjust our fresh daily clay prep batches accordingly.

do you offer private group events or studio rentals?

❯

absolutely! we host custom team buildings, birthday getaways, and private couples' sessions. experienced clay artists can also apply for our monthly open-studio passes.
